## Who I Am

I'm **Zaima Mashiat** — someone who lives at the intersection of **Artificial Intelligence, data, and building tech that actually helps people**. I enjoy exploring how AI can solve real-world problems, from data-driven insights to smarter, more impactful tools.

My work is rooted in creating systems that don't just work well — but work for people. Basically, I try to make computers smarter while hoping they don't realize they don't need me anymore.

---

## What I Do

My work spans building and experimenting with **AI & Machine Learning models**, working with **data analysis and automation**, developing **AI-powered solutions** for real-world challenges, and building software systems that create meaningful **social impact**.

My daily workflow usually looks something like this:

- Training models and analyzing data
- Debugging code for 2 hours and fixing it with one missing comma
- Optimizing systems so they run smarter and faster

---

## Tech & Tools

### Languages

I primarily work with **Python**, **Java**, and **Kotlin** — each suited to different corners of what I build.

### AI & Data

My core technical focus areas include **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **Data Analysis**, and building end-to-end **AI-driven systems**.

### Frameworks & Tools

My day-to-day toolkit includes **TensorFlow / PyTorch**, **React**, **Node.js**, **Git**, **Jupyter Notebook**, **Firebase**, **VS Code**, and **Android Studio**.

---

## Outside of Tech

When I'm not training models or chasing down bugs, I recharge by doing things that require **zero GPUs**:

- **Cooking** — experimenting with recipes like they're machine learning models
- **Painting** — because sometimes creativity needs pixels, sometimes paint
- **Gaming** — strategic thinking… or just losing to a boss fight repeatedly

> Cooking and machine learning are surprisingly similar. If you mix the wrong ingredients, things break in very interesting ways.

---

## Currently Exploring

Right now, I'm deep into **advanced AI systems**, **LLM applications**, **AI for social good**, and building smarter AI-powered tools that solve problems at scale.
